Ask Magic

A powerful AI right within your WhatsApp. Magic can recieve any attachment, search the internet, transcribe audios, generate images, create reminders and more. Ask Magic anything and become one of the thousand users of Magic typing below. Scroll down to learn more.

What can you do with Magic?

Send audios

When you're in a hurry, there's no need to type. Record an audio and Magic will listen and respond to it.

Send image, video or other files

You can send images, videos, or other types of attached documents to Magic for analysis and get responses related to them.

Search the internet

Magic can search the internet to provide answers about current events or to give better responses.

Deep search

If a regular search isn't enough and you want Magic to research extensively to answer your question, include /deepsearch in your message.

Generate images

Generate super-realistic images in various formats just by asking Magic!

Transcribe audio

When you're busy and someone sends you an audio message, forward it to Magic and it will transcribe the audio for you.

Create PDFs

Ask Magic to create a document or PDF for you. It will open in a markdown editor where you can make changes or collaborate with the AI on the document, and finally export it as .md or .pdf

Create events

Ask Magic to send you the temperature every morning or remind you to do a task, and it will send you a message at the time of the event.

Answer difficult questions

If you ask Magic about complex topics like math calculations, you can include /think in your message so Magic can work through a line of reasoning before answering.

Create stickers

Send images along with /sticker to transform them into 1:1 stickers.
